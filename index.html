Crystal Collector Game

How To Play The Game:

There are four crystals below. You will be given a Target Score at the start of the game. Each crystal's value is hidden until you click on it. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of number to match the Target Score given. You win the game by matching your total amount to the Total Score, you lose the game if your total amount goes above the Total Score. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
